About us

We are ISO 9001:2015 in QMS and ISO 14001:2015 in EMS and ISO 45001:2018 in OHS certified company established ourselves as one of the reputed organization engaged in designing manufacturing and providing services for all kind of steel structure. Our story began in 2002 the time when cell phone were the latest entrants in telecom sector. It was a challenge for country like India to provide wireless networks coverage across the nation. The work needed a great deal of knowledge and robust experience with the vision to building our nation into truly wirless Company .Our Director, Mr. Naushad Ali Siddiqui with wide experience In Telecom sector, ventured Aman Infra Developer: We are a one-stop for Indias telecom giants providing out engineering, designing, procurement and fabrication under one roof. AMAN INFRA DEVELOPER, takes pride in Offering its clients not only end to end telecom tower infrastructure related solutions with best quality product and services but also other infrastructural needs of fast developing nation.
